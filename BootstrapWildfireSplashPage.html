About

Wildfire is a web and mobile app that allows you to stay informed during bushfires. We collect all the information you need, and display it in an intuitive way. Users can report fire sightings, road blockages, and other hazards, creating a real-time map of how the fire is moving. Wildfire also helps you organise your bushfire preparation plans, evacuation points and emergency contacts, giving you all the information you need in one convinient app.
